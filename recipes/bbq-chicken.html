Baked BBQ Chicken

Grilled Chicken covered with delicous BBQ Sauce

This scrumptious version of sticky chicken took me years to perfect. But I am ready to release the recipe to the public. It takes about 10 minutes of prep, and 50 minutes to cook and yields 4 servings, perfect for the family.

Ingredients

    1/2 cup prepared barbecue sauce
    1/4 cup soy sauce
    1/4 cup red wine
    1/4 cup maple syrup
    1 tablespoon chili powder
    1 tablespoon dry mustard powder
    1 tablespoon garlic powder
    1 teaspoon onion powder
    1 teaspoon ground cumin
    4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
    1 tablespoon cornstarch
    1 cup water
